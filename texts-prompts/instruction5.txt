## JSON Format (use `sample-lineage.json` as your template)

Each family needs: `id`, `era`, `father`, `mother` (or `null`). Each person needs: `name`, `life`, `title`, `desc`, `image` (filename in `public/assets/`). 
Set `"isSubject": true` on the last entry.
